My name is Luka Neal. I’m 18 years old, I grew up in Downtown Houston, and I’ve loved tech since I was a little kid. Although I’ve spent most of my life in artistic atmospheres, I’ve always made a point to take things apart and figure out how they work down to their smallest parts. I’ve had experience with Python, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and I’m currently learning Swift for IOS development. Before I learned how to code, though, I learned the essentials of networking and hacking. Having set up countless virtual machines, I quickly realized within the last few years that this is exactly what I want to do. Computers have a certain allure for me, they always have. Learning how to take concepts or designs from the least organized parts of my brain and turn them into functions or websites is my favorite part of programming, and I hope I continue to do so for as long as I can.
